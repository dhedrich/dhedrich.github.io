About Me

Originally from Calgary, Canada, Donovan is a full-stack web developer(-in-training) working primarily with the MEAN and MERN stacks to produce robust and fully functional web applications. Donovan's creations offer a highly polished user experience by seamlessly integrating his affinity for bright, eye-catching colors and intricate geometric patterns into a responsive and intuitively designed application.

Based in San Clemente, CA, Donovan is a die-hard Lakers fan. He graduated from UCI with a B.S. in Chemistry. His hobbies include Super Smash Bros, powerlifting, spicy food challenges, and expanding his ever-growing knowledge of full-stack web development.
