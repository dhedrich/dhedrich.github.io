Boutique Memory CareOur compassion sets us apart

Our staff is specially trained to provide compassionate care and they continue to receive on-going training to ensure our high standards of care are met. Our various services include:

  • Serving mild to moderate dementia and those with special needs**
  • 24 hour care and supervision with awake night staff Personalized assistance with bathing, dressing, and grooming as well as other basic activities of daily living
  • Care and supervision for speech, hearing, and vision impaired resident
  • Total incontinent care of both bladder and bowel
  • Assistance and safety supervision with mobility using walkers, wheelchairs, and use of mechanical devices such as a Hoyer lift
  • Monitoring, management, and assistance with medications
  • Provision of three nutritious and well balanced meals plus snacks and assistance with feeding
  • Modify diet as necessary such as low salt, low sugar, pureed food, etc.
  • Daily housekeeping and laundry. Dry cleaning not included
  • Daily meaningful activities that fit the resident’s interests and functional capabilities
  • Hospice waiver available to provide continuity of care until end of life

** Optional care and services to meet special needs also available upon request

Villa Monticello Assisted Living is a 29-bed Escondido Assisted Living facility. We provide care for ambulatory or non-ambulatory seniors in various stages of dementia and frail elderly who may have physical and/or medical challenges that require full care and assistance.

At Villa Monticello Assisted Living, we recognize the need to provide individualized care for seniors who have either physical or mental challenges, and in some cases both. This requires a special knowledge, understanding, sensitivity, and commitment to meet their ever-changing level of needs for personal care and supervision.

Our goal and philosophy is centered around supporting the resident’s uniqueness and placing an emphasis on the whole person. Treating each resident with respect and dignity, as well as promoting overall wellbeing, are our top priorities.
